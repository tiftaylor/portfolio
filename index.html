About Tif Taylor

I'm a Full-Stack Software Developer with a background in people and office management and project coordination. I first learned about programming as I was finishing up my philosohpy degree and it caught my interest because of my natural love for logic. It made me excited to want to apply my logical thinking skills to application design and user experience.

In my free time, I am contributing as a developer on a team that is building an open-source festival registration web application designed to make an event accessible for all income levels using a tailored community based pricing system.

While I have superpowers like organization, planning and time management that enabled me to make team processes and workflows more effecient, as a developer, I am looking for opportunities at companies like Google, Apple or Facebook, that specifically put a lot of thought and care into their UI design.

My passion is to bring structure to ambiguity and clarity to confusion, and I want to flex my strengths and apply analytic thought-work to help consturct clear, clean and confident user experiences.
